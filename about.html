About Girigreen

Giri Green Driving School Ltd is a limited liability company incorporated under the Companies Act (Cap.486), on 29th January 2014. It’s an accredited and licensed NTSA institution for the training of drivers and riders, per NTSA School ID No. DSLR-AAA0AGD1.

The school was established to enhance the quality of driver training in Kenya and reduce road accidents through expert instruction, outreach programs, and safety campaigns. We are fully equipped to produce safe, skilled, and confident drivers.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To offer comprehensive and impactful driver training aimed at reducing accidents caused by driver negligence — all at an affordable cost.

Our Vision

To be an exemplary driving school recognized nationally and globally for producing safe and responsible drivers.
